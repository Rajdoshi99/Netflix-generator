The Project

Great Music Taste

Netflix-Text-Generator is a fine tuned GPT-2 model that learned over 8k Netflix descriptions.

Interactive

Netflix-Text-Generator will generate descriptions based off of any specific prompt related to a plot provided.

Timeline

Netflix-Text-Generator was completed in just 9 days along with the development, training and deployment.

Extensions

Some extensions to this project include generate new sentences based on genre, director, year. Further training on Big-data could improve performance!
